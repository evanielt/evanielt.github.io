Multiplayer Shooty Game

Originally, this was going to be a sort of tf2 inspired game with classes and quick matches. The idea behind the game has changed so many times since then. Every time I wanted to work on a new project, such as a tank simulator or an airplane, I just invisioned a way to merge it into MSG. It's now becoming a sort of blend between ravenfield and dcs. The freedom that dcs has to offer with the customizability of ravenfield. idk if it'll ever get finished, scope creep's real bro
I've been inspired by countless games over the years and I'd like to combine all the best parts into something that I would have fun with. I don't want to have the complexity of DCS in order to play with friends who aren't into learning a plane completely, but I don't want it to be as simple as say project reality. A VTOL VR or Derail Valley -like experience in terms of complexity seems perfect.
